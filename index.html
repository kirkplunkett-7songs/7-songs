About Us

Founded in 2018, 7 Songs brings ancient healing traditions to modern wellness. Our certified sound therapists combine years of experience in music therapy, meditation, and holistic health to create transformative healing experiences.

We believe in the profound power of vibration to restore balance to mind, body, and spirit. Each session is carefully crafted to meet you where you are on your wellness journey.

Our practitioners are certified by the Sound Healing Association with advanced training in therapeutic sound techniques, ensuring you receive the highest quality care in a safe, nurturing environment.
